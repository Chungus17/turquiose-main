Who Are We

At Turquoise, we are committed to delivering top-tier engineering, logistics, and HR solutions

Established in 2017 in the State of Kuwait, TURQUOISE has emerged over the years to become a recognized provider of a wide range of support services for its diverse clientele in Qatar, Kuwait, Turkiye, Pakistan and other GCC countries.

The company's reputation is centered on its capability to provide high quality and professional HR solutions, most notably in the fields of local and overseas manpower, Contracting and engineering services, Import & Export, Trading Services and Construction Materials supply.

Our mission is to establish ourselves as the leading provider of Facilities Services, Electrical & Mechanical Engineering Constructions, Heavy Equipment Rental &Leasing, Maintenance, Import & Export, Trading Services, turn key HR solutions for Governmental Contracts, Commercial sectors, Oil & Gas, Refineries, Municipalities & International organizations.
